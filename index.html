📜 Game Rules & Terms of Service

⚠️ Zero Tolerance Policy
Any violation of these rules will result in an immediate and permanent ban of your account. There are no warnings. Banned accounts will not be restored.
1. Exploiting Bugs & Glitches

The intentional use of any bug, glitch, or unintended game mechanic to gain an unfair advantage is strictly forbidden. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Duplicating items, gold, crystals, or any in-game resource
  • Bypassing cooldowns, rate limits, or other game restrictions
  • Manipulating game state through browser console, modified requests, or scripts
  • Exploiting arena, trading, or egg mechanics in unintended ways
  • Using any method to gain stats, XP, or resources faster than intended
2. Bug Reporting

If you discover a bug or exploit, you are required to report it immediately. Players who discover and report bugs in good faith will not be punished. Players who knowingly abuse a bug instead of reporting it will be permanently banned.

3. Community Conduct

Poke Online is a welcoming community for all players. The following behavior is strictly forbidden in chat, trainer names, and all interactions:

  • Insults, harassment, bullying, or personal attacks against other players
  • Racism, sexism, homophobia, or any form of discrimination
  • Hate speech, slurs, or derogatory language of any kind
  • Threats of violence, doxxing, or sharing personal information of others
  • Sexual, explicit, or otherwise inappropriate content
  • Spamming, flooding, or deliberately disrupting chat
  • Impersonating staff, moderators, or other players
  • Advertising, scamming, or phishing attempts

Treat others with respect. If you wouldn't say it to someone's face — don't type it.

4. Real Money Trading (RMT)

Selling, buying, or trading any in-game content (accounts, Pokemon, gold, items, crystals) for real money or any form of external compensation is strictly prohibited. This includes:

  • Selling or buying accounts, Pokemon, or in-game currency for real money
  • Trading in-game goods for items or services in other games
  • Offering or accepting any form of payment outside the official shop

The only way to obtain premium currency is through the official in-game Crystal Shop.

5. Multi-Accounting

Each player is allowed one account. Creating multiple accounts to gain any kind of advantage is forbidden — this includes but is not limited to farming resources, manipulating the market, boosting rankings, abusing referral or event systems, or trading between your own accounts.

6. Fair Play

Poke Online is designed to be fair for all players. Any attempt to circumvent game systems, manipulate the economy, or gain advantages through methods not intended by the developers is a violation of these rules. The use of bots, macros, or automated scripts that play the game for you is not allowed.

7. Account Responsibility

You are responsible for all activity on your account. Sharing your account or allowing others to access it is at your own risk. Only one active session per account is allowed at any time. All purchases made through the Crystal Shop are final — filing fraudulent payment disputes or chargebacks will result in an immediate and permanent ban.

8. Enforcement

The Poke Online team reserves the right to ban any account at any time for any reason. All bans are final. Game data, including gold, items, Pokemon, and progress, may be reset or removed at the discretion of the administrators.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Introduction

Poke Online MMORPG ("we", "our", or "us") is a browser-based multiplayer Pokemon game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our game.

Information We Collect

Account Data: When you register, we store your chosen trainer name and password (encrypted with bcrypt). No real name or email address is required to play.

Game Data: Your trainer progress, Pokemon collection, inventory, stats, and in-game actions are stored on our servers to provide the game experience.

Technical Data: We may collect basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and device type for security and performance purposes.

Payment Data: If you make a purchase, payment processing is handled by PayPal. We do not store your payment details — only a transaction ID and the items purchased.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to provide and improve the game experience, maintain game security, prevent cheating, and fix bugs.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted passwords, JWT authentication, rate limiting, and input validation to protect your information. Game data is retained as long as your account is active.

Children's Privacy

Poke Online is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, all your game data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
